A Poster Design Competition

    TOPICS:

  • Staying safe on the internet.
  • Reducing your Digital Footprint.
  • Wilcard- Any poster that spreads "awareness about technology".

    • RULES:

  • You can use any software to create posters. Bonus points will be awarded for use of Photoshop, Illustrator or similar software. You can use any of the templates available below.
  • All posters must be mailed to acm.svcecse@gmail.com with the subject "Poster Design". Please include your Name, College, Year and Department in the body of the mail. If you have a work file (.psd, .ai, .cd, etc.), attach the same.
  • You may submit more than one poster.
  • You may submit posters before midnight on 15th March 2019.
  • All posters will be put up on our social media pages. The most popular posters will receive bonus points.
  • The best posters may be printed for display on our college noticeboards.
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited. You may use images and text from elsewhere but the overall design of the poster must be your own.

    • JUDGING CRITERIA:

  • Artistry & Creativity
  • Overall Appeal
  • Relevance & Adherence to theme
  • Originality & Uniqueness

    • PRIZES:

  • 1st Place:₹300; 2nd place:₹200.
  • Digital Certificates for winners.
  • Selected SVCE students maybe invited to interview for design roles at the SVCE ACM STUDENT CHAPTER
    and Curtain Call SVCE (Drama Club)
